This guide shows how to bridge AWS FSx services with cloud-based identity management by using Entra Domain Services as a managed domain controller. A site-to-site VPN connects AWS and Azure, enabling Entra ID users to authenticate against FSx SMB shares without maintaining on-premises infrastructure.
